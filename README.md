## RemindToDeleteMedia Privacy Policy 📱🔒

**Official Privacy Policy Repository** for the **RemindToDeleteMedia** Android application.

This repository is maintained for:
- **Public access**: a stable link to the privacy policy
- **Google Play Store**: privacy policy URL for your listing
- **Transparency**: clear explanation of data handling

### About RemindToDeleteMedia
RemindToDeleteMedia helps you manage storage by setting reminders to review media folders. The app **never deletes media automatically** — you delete manually in your gallery/file manager.
